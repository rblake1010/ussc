GLOBAL SERVICE SUPPORT


Servicing the Best Customers in the World!

United States Sales Corporation maintains category specific Account Executives who are highly trained in their assigned classification. Each Account Executive is supported by an administrative assistant as well as our sales and support team. Additionally, USSC employs a worldwide sales force comprised of dedicated professionals who support the continued growth of our brands, as well as our vital sales and servicing relationships at store level.

U.S. Military Exchange Stores are serviced in 15 countries, wherever our U.S. Military personnel are stationed around the globe (see geography in red below). These AAFES, NEX and MCX resale facilities are frequented every one to four weeks, and some larger stores daily within the following countries:

• USA • PUERTO RICO • CUBA • UNITED KINGDOM • GERMANY
• NETHERLANDS • SPAIN • ITALY • TURKEY • GREECE • BAHRAIN
• SAUDI ARABIA • GUAM • KOREA • JAPAN
(Note: products also distributed to Iraq and Afghanistan, however,
no store service is authorized under DOD safety guidelines).
