REPRESENTATION

Our in-store sales and service support includes Merchandising, Training, Replenishment and Demonstrations (MTRD). The Military Exchange Market is dependent upon its retail partners to provide a significant amount of store level hours to support the product positioning and sell-through of each brand. This includes ensuring our manufacturers are represented at "Show-and-Sell" events, all Headquarters sponsored/in-store product training venues, New Store openings, and all other Special Sale Event opportunities.
