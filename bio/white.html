Cathy White, Exchange Sales Manager / Director of Field Personnel
Email Cathy White

Education
Graduated 10th in the Class of ‘79 At Brentsville High School, VA
President, National Honor Society & Future Business Leaders of America
Completed some community college courses (Opted for a “Mrs” degree!)

Background
United States Sales Corp: (1981 - 1989)
Was fortunate to be a “stay-at-home” mom for a decade, while active in PTA & Girl Scouts – worked 5 yrs for the Chesapeake Elections Dept, before returning full-time to U.S. Sales in early 2006.

Professional Experience
Cathy has worked in every department of U.S. Sales during her tenure and understands our mission to servicing our manufacturers and military exchanges, worldwide. She is the “head-cheerleader” for our sales and service support team, and is passionate about every aspect of her job.

Prior Experience
Cathy worked as a civil-service employee at an Army base in northern Virginia after graduation, before moving to Virginia Beach and joining our corporate team. Her enthusiasm opened doors to advancement and inter-office transfers, giving her a full spectrum of working knowledge of our industry.

Other Activities
Cathy is an active member of Immanuel Baptist Church as well as Immanuel Christian Learning Center volunteer.

Other Personal Information
Cathy was raised in a military family and her son is now a member of the U.S. Navy. She is the mother of two, grandmother of one, and wife of 30+ yrs. She has resided in Tidewater since 1981.
