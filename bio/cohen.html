Nathaniel J. “Nick” Cohen, Vice President / Marketing
Email Nick Cohen

Education
Norfolk Academy - Graduate (1960)
Washington & Lee University (B.A., 1964)
Marshall-Wythe School of the College of William & Mary (J.D., 1968)

Background
United States Sales Corporation (1982 to Present)
Law Practice (1969-1982)

Experience at U.S. Sales
Hired as Director of Sales to organize sales force, develop exchange coverage strategies and help develop a meaningful, workable portfolio. Appointed V.P. Sales. Appointed V.P. Marketing. Helped develop concept that fewer portfolio members can mean greater return on investment for active portfolio and this corporation. Provide assistance in identifying potential candidates for association with this company, and then assisted in creating focused marketing and sales strategies for the members of my portfolio team. Have formerly represented footwear and housewares vendors, now specializing in electronics and sporting goods.

Prior Experience
Worked summers and Holidays in family-owned Sporting Goods business (distributors/retailers) while in high school and college. Assisted in the development of a multi-city/multi-State retail chain of sporting goods stores.

Other Activities
Board Member of the Hampton Roads American Logistic Association (HRALA), this industry’s trade association. Avid American Muscle Car enthusiast, especially 1958-62 Corvettes. Fishing, both offshore and fly-fishing. Antique collecting. Mountain biking.

Other Personal Information
Married to Wife for nearly 50 years, and proud father of two, and three Grandchildren. He is a native of Virginia Beach, VA.
