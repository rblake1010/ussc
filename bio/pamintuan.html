Professional Experience
Ariel Pamintuan was appointed Western Zone Manager / Trainer as a USSC full-time employee in July 2014. His skill-set includes management of store personnel, scheduling/ planning, delegating tasks to employees and time management. His past expertise has been focused upon driving business within the Electronics Dept. He has now expanded his product category and brand knowledge so as to become accountable to manage the success for our entire portfolio of brands.

Prior Experience
Ariel’s background includes 17 years Electronics sales experience which included key sales positions for JVC Electronics programs and Apple products within the San Diego Military Exchange marketplace. Having also worked for the Marine Corps Exchange in San Diego, he has a firsthand knowledge of how they operate, as well as their expectations of the independent rep agencies which support the stores.

Further Mention:
An opportunity was created for one of our recent (USSC) San Diego based Electronics Sales Reps (ESRs) to step up to the plate and begin managing the Western half of the United States and Far East as our Western Zone Manager / Trainer. Those of you on our Electronics Team, who have visited the San Diego area military stores and/or trade show expos, are already familiar with Ariel. He has been the leader of our “West Coast Dream Team” for the past few years. Ariel has exhibited exemplary communication and reporting skills, as well as planning & execution of store events. He has hosted visits from our manufacturers and buyers and succeeded in leaving nothing but the utmost professional impression. We are always pleased to have the opportunity to “promote from within the ranks”. USSC is excited to have Ariel join our middle-management team.
