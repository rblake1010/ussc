David Brophy, Electronics, Account Executive
Email David Brophy

Education
Princess Anne High School,
Graduated 1989 Tidewater Community College 1992,
Associates Degree- Criminal Justice

Background
United States Sales Corp: Account Executive (2015 to Present)
Navy Exchange Divisional Manager, Norfolk Navy Base (2014 - 2015)
EURPAC Warehouse Sales: Account Executive (2000 - 2014)

Professional Experience
David was brought on to the U.S. Sales team to help manage and develop our electronics portfolio. His current assigned responsibility includes Garmin, Yamaha, Xentris, Olympus, and Gearhead. David is also working in a supportive role in managing our in-store service and field support programs.

Prior Experience
David’s background is extensively within the Military Resale System. Throughout his 14 years at EURPAC he directed the Ships Stores Afloat program. He was promoted to Director of Sales, and ultimately appointed as an Account Executive. David called upon the Hqtrs buyers at NEXCOM, the Marine Corps, and Coast Guard. David has extensive field knowledge, a tremendous Electronics background, and experienced in product development. Soon after EURPAC, David managed the Electronics department in the World’s Largest Navy Exchange (in Norfolk VA) which drove in over a $100 million in retail sales.

Other Activities
David is actively engaged in his children’s sports and academic programs. He is an avid runner, having participated in four marathons

Other Personal Information
David was born and raised in the Military Community; son of a Retired Warrant Officer (CWO 4) in the Navy. David grew up on military bases both in the United States and overseas. He is happily married to his wife, Suzy, for 17years, and has twins that are very active and support his busy travel lifestyle.
