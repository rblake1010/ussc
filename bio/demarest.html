David Demarest, Account Executive, Electronics
Email David Demarest

Education
Coastal Carolina, Graduated 1998
Associates of Arts Degree
Minor Studies in Criminal Justice

Background
United States Sales Corp: Account Executive (2010 to Present)
JVC USA Corporation: National Military Sales Manager (2002 - 2010)
Kenwood USA Corporation: Military Sales Manager (1997 - 2002)
Woofer ‘n’ Tweeter: Sales Specialist (1992 - 1997)

Professional Experience
David was brought on to the U.S. Sales team to help manage and develop our electronics portfolio. He is currently assigned responsibility for LG Electronics & Garmin, two of the agency‘s largest brands. David is also working in a supportive role in managing our in-store field staff to better coordinate the information and training of the ever changing electronics category.

Prior Experience
David’s background is extensively within the Military Resale System, directing the Sales and Marketing side for two major electronic companies over the last thirteen years. David also has vast field knowledge and strong relationships with both Navy and Marine Corps store personnel where he has been a pivotal component of JVC’s training and oversight. Our Military Exchange buyers were extremely supportive of David’s appointment in joining USSC.

Other Activities
David is actively engaged in his local community with youth sports and the local scouting organization.

Other Personal Information
David was born and raised in the Military Community, son of a Retired Marine Corps Master Sergeant. He is happily married to his wife, Johnsie, and has two great kids who are all very supportive of his busy travel lifestyle.
