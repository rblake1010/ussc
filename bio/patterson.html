Professional Experience
Debbie credits her varied background in perfecting her skills to serve as an AAFES Account Executive. Starting with United States Sales Corporation in 2005, she currently represents food/confections and furniture/home furnishings to the various AAFES retail and catalog buyers. These different categories call upon her background in food and confections which enable her to work with the food buyers as well as her background in fashion which enables her to stay on top of fast moving trends in furniture and home furnishings. She is involved in all aspects of her category business such as new item presentations, plan o gram placement, seasonal presentations, advertising coordination, marketing programs and special event planning with AAFES worldwide.

Prior Experience
Debbie’s background includes 12 years in the fashion industry in the areas of sales, merchandising, and buying. After several years of caring for her young daughter, she returned to the work place as an account executive with a major rep agency specializing in food/confections. She grew the business for all trade classes including mass, grocery, convenience, and distributor which provided an extensive background for her work with AAFES.

Other Personal Information
Debbie is married to James Patterson, and they have three children, Mandy, Cherilyn, and Brad, all of whom reside in the Dallas metropolis.
