Michael Chapdelaine, Senior Zone Manager / Trainer
Email Mike Chapdelaine

Education
Liberty University
Bachelor of Science Degree
Business Management (June 2008)

Background
United States Sales Corp: (2006 - Present)
United States Navy (1984 - 2006)

Professional Experience
Michael was appointed as the USSC Hampton Roads Regional Manager in October 2006. In October 2007 he was promoted to Eastern Zone Manager/Trainer to increase our sales agency’s exposure and service support for our portfolio of brands to all AAFES, Navy and Marine Corps Exchanges East of the Mississippi and in Europe. This includes direct in-store training and evaluation of our In-Store Representative (ISR) network. In 2014, Mike was promoted to Senior Zone Manager/ Trainer with oversight over our global Zone Manager network.

Prior Experience
Michael’s background includes twenty-two years working for NEXCOM (Ship’s Store Division) in the United States Navy. During this time he managed numerous personnel in various positions both onboard ships and shore installations. He also has experience in facilities management and sales. His final duty station was at the Afloat Training Group where he conducted shipboard inspections, record audits, and training. He retired as a Navy Chief.

Other Activities
Michael, and his wife Janice, are involved in the Virginia Beach Department of Human Services Foster Care Program and have adopted 2 girls, Myah and Kaylee.
