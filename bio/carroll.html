Professional Experience
Jim was brought on to deliver the product information and training needed to keep our reps at the top of their game and to insure that the brands we represent in the electronics arena are maintained at a high level.

Jim is an avid amateur radio operator having been first licensed in 1965. Other hobbies include stamp and watch collecting. Jim has also written freelance articles and has been published in several hobbyist magazines.

Other Personal Information
Jim was born and raised in the greater Philadelphia area, is married to his wife, Claudette, and is the proud dad of 3 daughters. He currently resides in Glen Allen, Va.
