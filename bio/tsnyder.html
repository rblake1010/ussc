Professional Experience
United States Sales Corporation has launched a dedicated ‘Fashion Division’ under the direction of Tricia Snyder. Her experience includes 25 years in the apparel business, in both wholesale and retail venues, as well as prior years selling fashion merchandise to all branches of the Military Exchange System. Her background includes working directly with Ralph Lauren, Norma Kamali, Merona Sport, and Nancy Heller in both merchandising and senior sales management capacities. For the last 20 years, Tricia has operated her own retail stores in Virginia Beach and Aspen.

We look forward to this expansion to our sales agency’s portfolio, with a dedicated commitment to further grow our business within this more specialized product classification, complimented by Trisha’s leadership skills and strong sense of fashion expertise.

It is our desire to introduce to the Military Exchange Market nationally known brands which are not only distributed to major department stores and sporting goods chains, but also have the opportunity to create some real excitement on the sales floor. The exchange systems have announced to industry their desire to expand its assortments to include better branded resources which already enjoy ‘retail’ success, and, therefore, we have identified and secured several “hipper” national brands to begin our launch.

Other personal information
Tricia has a son, Cody. Her interests are golf, skiing, travel, and adventure, as well as a daily workout routine to maintain good balance in life.
