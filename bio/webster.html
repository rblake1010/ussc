Justin Webster, Account Executive - Athletic Footwear
Email Justin Webster

Education
Southern Methodist University, Graduated 1991
BBA Finance

Background
United States Sales Corp (2005 to Present)
New Balance: Independent Sales Rep (2002 - 2004)
MVP Sports: Independent Sales Rep (2000 - 2002)
ASICS America: Independent Sales Rep (1995 - 1999)
Ross Products Division: Account Executive (1992 - 1994)

Professional Experience
Justin’s sixteen years of footwear and apparel experience has helped grow ASICS to the #1 athletic footwear vendor in AAFES exchanges and the #1 footwear vendor on AAFES.com. He works closely with the buyers to forecast sales, plan marketing / promotional events and create each season’s assortment plan.

Prior Experience
Justin’s nineteen years of sales experience working with major retailers such as J.C. Penney, Dillard’s, Academy Sports, Foot Action and Olympia Sports has enabled him to understand the challenges within each trade channel while identifying growth opportunities for each brand he represents. ASICS has bestowed the sales excellence award to Justin from ’04 through ’09.

Other Activities
Justin is an avid runner and has completed the New York City Marathon twice and the Boston Marathon once.

Other Personal Information
Justin is married to Kim Webster. They have two young boys and live in Dallas.
