Jennifer "Jenn" Schreck, Account Executive
Email Jenn Schreck

Education
High School Graduate, Tokyo, Japan

Background
United States Sales Corp: Account Executive (July 2013 to present)
JRS Worldwide- Category Manager…Competitive MILREP agency responsible for Military Exchange sales for the Under Armour account. (2008-2013)
NEXCOM Hqtrs- Virginia Beach VA – Assistant Buyer – Men’s and Women’s Buying Team (2002-2008)
NEXCOM Hqtrs- Virginia Beach VA - Merchandiser for cosmetics, watches, costume and fine jewelry. Navy Exchange- Atsugi, Japan

Prior Experience
Jenn is the product of a military father who spent 26 years in the Navy. She had an opportunity most people dream about; growing up overseas. Jenn lived in Japan for 6 years, where she worked at the Atsugi (NEX) Base Exchange. After moving back to America, she began working immediately at a local bookstore in Virginia Beach. After 5 years, she returned to the exchange system, working as a merchandiser.She was promoted to assistant buyer after 1 year and worked with the ladies and men's buying teams. Her main role in ladies was buying the Under Armour category. After 6 years with the Navy Exchange, she was recruited to JRS Worldwide as a category manager, selling Under Armour to the Navy, Marines, and Coast Guard buying offices, where she remained for 5 years.

Current Job Responsibilites
Jenn manages a very defined portfolio for the agency, to include our Watch (E.GLUCK) brands,and calls upon the Hqtrs Buying Offices of AAFES, NEXCOM, Marine Corps, and the Coast Guard.

Other Activities
Jenn lives in Virginia Beach. She loves spending time with her family and friends. She enjoys traveling, fashion, concerts, the sun, water, photography, meeting people and learning new cultures. Jenn is dynamic and passionate about her work, and she is a significant asset to our Acc't Executive Team.
